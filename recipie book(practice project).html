PAV BHAJI:

Pav Bhaji is a popular Indian street food that originated in Mumbai. It's a flavorful, spicy vegetable mash (bhaji) served with soft buttered bread rolls (pav). The dish is loved for its combination of rich spices and the buttery texture of the pav, making it a favorite snack or meal.
